If you need to use this application in a remote area without internet access, please download the OperationSmile data collection app, click here to learn more.

Tutorial

  • 1. Access the Microsoft Store: Open the Microsoft Store on your Android or iOS phone or access this link.

  • 2. Search for the App: Tap on the search bar and type 'Power Apps'. Find and tap on the application in the list of results.

  • 3. Install the App: On the Power Apps page, tap 'Install'.

  • 4. Wait for the Download: The app will be downloaded and installed automatically. Follow the progress in the notification bar.

  • 5. Open the App: After installation, find the Operation Smile icon in your app list and tap it to start using.

  • 6. Use the app every time you need to collect data without internet access.
